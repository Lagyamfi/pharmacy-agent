# Pharmacy Support Agent

Welcome! This AI-powered pharmacy assistant runs in two modes — switch between them via the **Chat Settings** panel (gear icon ⚙️).

## Modes

| Mode | Description |
|---|---|
| **💊 Customer Support** | Order tracking, product availability, Rx safety warnings, invoicing, FDA drug data, and medication education |
| **🔍 Admin Analyst** | Ask natural-language questions that are converted to SQL and run against the pharmacy database |

## What you can ask

**Orders**
- *"What's the status of order ORD-101?"*
- *"Show all my orders — my name is Kwame Asante"*
- *"I'd like to cancel order ORD-102"*

**Products & Inventory**
- *"Do you have Augmentin?"* — searches by partial name or brand
- *"What malaria drugs do you carry?"* — browse by therapeutic category
- *"Is tramadol available?"* — includes Rx and controlled drug warnings
- *"Ampicillin is out of stock — what else do you have?"* — suggests alternatives

**Invoicing**
- *"I'd like 2x ampicillin susp and 1x paracetamol syrup"* — generates a PDF invoice in GHS
- Upload a **prescription image or PDF** and the agent will extract medications, check availability, and prepare your invoice automatically

**Drug Information**
- *"What is metronidazole used for?"*
- *"Can I take co-trimoxazole and metronidazole together?"*
- *"What are the FDA warnings for chloramphenicol?"*

## Voice Mode

Click the **microphone** button to speak your question. The agent will transcribe your speech (via Gemini) and respond with both text and audio.

⚠️ *This assistant provides information only. Always consult a qualified pharmacist or doctor for medical advice.*
